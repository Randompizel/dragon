Interest Rates
How Interest Works

Interest rates in Lend.Meme are dynamic and adjust automatically based on market conditions. The primary factor determining rates is pool utilization - as more tokens are borrowed, rates increase to incentivize more deposits.

üìä Supply APY

The interest rate earned by suppliers, derived from borrower interest payments and pool utilization.

üí∏ Borrow APY

The interest rate paid by borrowers, which increases with higher pool utilization.

Liquidations
How Liquidations Work

Liquidations protect protocol solvency when borrowers' positions become undercollateralized. Your current "Borrow Balance" and "Supply Balance" are calculated using two oracles: Pyth (main) and Switchboard (backup).

üéØ Health Factor

Visible in your Account Details screen. When the Health Bar reaches the orange line (Liquidation Threshold), your account becomes eligible for liquidation.

‚öñÔ∏è Liquidation Threshold

Calculated based on the weighted average of all your deposited assets' individual thresholds.

Liquidation Process
üìä

When your position becomes eligible for liquidation:
‚Ä¢Third Party Liquidators repay 20% of your loans
‚Ä¢They receive equivalent collateral plus 5% bonus
‚Ä¢The 5% bonus serves as a bounty to secure the protocol

Frequently Asked Questions
General Questions
‚ùì What is Lend.Meme?

Lendragon.org is the first decentralized lending protocol specifically designed for meme tokens on Solana. Users can supply meme tokens to earn interest or use them as collateral for borrowing.

üîí Are my tokens safe?

The protocol will be audited by OtterSec and implements multiple security measures including multi-sig governance, conservative risk parameters, and dual oracle systems. However, as with any DeFi protocol, users should understand the risks involved..

üí∞ How do I earn interest?

Supply your meme tokens to the protocol to start earning interest. The APY varies based on market conditions and pool utilization. You'll also earn additional $MEME rewards for participating.

Supply & Borrow
üìà How are interest rates determined?

Interest rates are dynamic and based on pool utilization. Higher utilization leads to higher rates to encourage more deposits and fewer borrowers. Lower utilization results in lower rates to encourage borrowing.

üè¶ What is collateral?

Collateral is the tokens you supply that secure your borrowed positions. Different tokens have different collateral factors that determine how much you can borrow against them.

‚ö° Can I withdraw anytime?

Yes, you can withdraw your supplied tokens at any time as long as you're not using them as collateral for an active borrow position and there's sufficient liquidity in the pool.

Risk & Liquidation
‚ö†Ô∏è What is liquidation?

Liquidation occurs when your health factor drops below 1. When liquidated, 20% of your borrowed amount is repaid by liquidators who receive your collateral at a 5% discount.

üéØ How do I avoid liquidation?

Monitor your health factor regularly, maintain a safe buffer above 1, and adjust your position by either adding more collateral or reducing borrowed amounts when needed.

üìä What is health factor?

Health factor is a metric that indicates how safe your borrowed position is from liquidation. It's based on your collateral value versus borrowed amount. Stay above 1 to avoid liquidation.

Technical
üîÑ How are prices determined?

We use two oracle systems - Pyth as primary and Switchboard as backup - to ensure accurate and reliable price feeds for all supported tokens.

üíé What are cTokens?

When you supply tokens, you receive cTokens that represent your deposit. These tokens automatically earn interest through their exchange rate with the underlying asset.

üõ†Ô∏è Which wallets are supported?

We support all major Solana wallets including Phantom, Solflare, and other browser extensions. Make sure you have enough SOL for transaction fees.
